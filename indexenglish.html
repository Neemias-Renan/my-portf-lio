Casco - Conservation of sea turtles

The platform (Casco) was created in 2020 with the objective of helping biologists monitor sea turtles and make the data collected available for future scientific research. The project is maintained by the student Neemias Renan, oriented by Prof. Me. Pedro Baesse and originally developed at IFRN.
